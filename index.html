Privacy Policy — Seismic Boutique

Last updated: July 6, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Seismic Boutique mobile application ("the App", "we", "us") handles information when you use it.

1. Information We Collect

Project inputs (on-device only). The App lets you enter engineering project data (project name, location, engineer name, and calculation parameters such as ground acceleration, soil type, and damping ratio). This data is stored locally on your device only and is not transmitted to us or to any third party.

Purchase information. When you buy the one-time "Lifetime Unlock", the purchase is processed entirely by Google Play Billing (or Samsung In-App Purchase, depending on where you downloaded the App). We do not collect or store your payment details (card numbers, billing address, etc.) — the store handles this in accordance with its own privacy policy. We only receive a purchase confirmation token used to unlock the App's features on your device.

Diagnostic data. If you use the "Restore Purchases" feature, the App communicates with the store's servers to verify your purchase. No personal profile or usage analytics are collected by us.

2. How We Use Information

  • To unlock the features you've purchased.
  • To let you save and reuse your project inputs locally on your device.

We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not sell or share your data with third parties.

3. Data Storage & Retention

All project data stays on your device (via local app storage) until you delete it or uninstall the App. We do not operate a server that stores your project data.

4. Third-Party Services

The App uses Google Play Billing and/or Samsung In-App Purchase to process the in-app purchase, depending on which store distributed your copy of the App. Each store's handling of payment and account data is governed by that store's own privacy policy:

  • Google Play Privacy Policy
  • Samsung Privacy Policy

5. Children's Privacy

The App is an engineering calculation tool intended for professional and educational use and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

6. Your Rights

Since project data is stored only on your device, you can delete it at any time by clearing the App's data or uninstalling the App. For questions about a store purchase, refer to that store's own support channels.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted with a new "Last updated" date. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the revised policy.
